📐 How the analysis works (and its limits)
FormCheck uses on-device AI pose tracking (no video leaves your phone). It measures the angles & timing it can see from a 2D side view — elbow, knee, set point, release timing vs your jump, tempo, balance and consistency across reps. Arc angle and release height are estimates shown as cues, not lab-exact. Film square side-on for honest numbers.
