📐 How the analysis works (and its limits)
FormCheck uses on-device AI pose tracking (no video leaves your phone). Every number is tagged MEASURED (reliable from a square side view: elbow & knee angles, trunk lean, set point, tempo — per published pose-accuracy studies) or EST (estimates: exact release-instant values, jump height, arc). If a joint wasn't visible enough on a rep, FormCheck says "not measured" instead of guessing. Targets come from published shooting biomechanics (measured pro release is ~160°, not a locked-out 180°; knee load ~60°; Noah's 45° rim-entry data). Film square side-on, whole body in frame, for the most honest numbers.
