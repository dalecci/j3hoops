The evaluation

Run it in division blocks

  • Each division gets its own 2-hour block (3–5, 6–8, 9–12) — evaluators focus, the gym stays calm.
  • Numbered pinnies: every kid is a number, not a guess.
  • Score straight into your tryout app for instant rankings.

Score six things, 1–10

  • Ball-handling · Shooting · Athleticism
  • Defense · Basketball IQ
  • Coachability / Effort — weight this; it’s your culture filter.

Tier, don’t cut

If a group runs deep, split into a competitive + a development team — same culture, same plan. At a new club, the kid you cut is the family you lose forever; the kid you develop is next year’s starter.

Your app earns its keep

With 50 kids you can’t rank from memory. Pinnie number → rubric score → ranked list per group → drag into teams.

Parent track — where buy-in is won

The 10–12 min vision talk

  • Who we are — the Jaguars, and why you’re here.
  • What we stand for — culture & development first.
  • What to expect — season, structure, communication, cost.
  • The ask — sign the contract, volunteer, sponsor, spread the word.
  • Q&A — 3 minutes, then point them to the tables.

Back it with paper

  • 1-page parent handbook.
  • Sign-up sheets: volunteer / sponsor / interest.
  • The parent contract, ready to sign.

The “go big” layer

Photo / video

Headshots at the backdrop + a hype recap reel. Becomes your social content and next year’s promo — highest-ROI spend.

Giveaway

Every kid leaves with something — wristband, sticker pack, or drawstring bag. Cheap, huge for the “real program” feel.

Merch

Have gear to pre-sell; take orders even if stock is thin.

Food + sponsors

Free water minimum; snack/coffee table or food truck. Line up 2–4 local sponsors for banners and tables.

Contingencies

Heat / no AC

Free water everywhere, breaks between waves.

Injury

First-aid staffed; emergency-contact binder at check-in.

More than 50 show up

Overflow pinnies + one spare evaluator ready.

Walk-ups

Keep day-of check-in open even though reg is live.

Device / app fails

Paper rubric sheets as backup.

Lost child

A named point person + a stated protocol at check-in.
