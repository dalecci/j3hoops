J3 HOOPS

Quest 3 Trainer · Phase 0

How to play

1

Squeeze the grip button to summon the ball to that hand.

2

Wind up and swing your arm — then let go of the grip to release the shot. The ball flies with your real hand speed.

3

Drain it through the hoop for a make. Build a streak.

4

Squeeze again any time to get a fresh ball.

Checking your headset…
Stand a few steps back, face the hoop, and give yourself room to swing.
